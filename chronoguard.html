ChronoGuard

The Guardian of the Timeline

Lore

The ChronoGuard is an ancient construct that watches over the timeline of the world. Every block placed, every block broken, every action leaves an echo in time. The ChronoGuard does not judge — it remembers. And when needed, it can return to any moment… and reveal the truth.

Project Vision

ChronoGuard is designed to track, record, and reconstruct events inside a world or server environment. It is more than a basic log system: it is a structured timeline of what happened, when it happened, and who caused it.
